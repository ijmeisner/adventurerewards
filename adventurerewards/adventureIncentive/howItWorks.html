How It Works!

1. Take Your Picture

On your favorite Social Media, post a picture of your adventure and use the special hashtag!

3. Rack Up Points

More adventures = More Points. After each picture you post, you are given an amount of points for your adventure.

Browse the different offers to decide which deal you want to get. With Adventure Rewards, you can expect exclusive deals.
